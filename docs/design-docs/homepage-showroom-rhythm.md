# Homepage Showroom Rhythm

## Goal
Make the public homepage feel like a dynamic showroom of notes rather than a product landing page.

## Composition Rules
- note previews are the primary content, not hero copy
- introductory framing stays present but visually secondary
- the grid should feel dynamic through card-height variation, not through chaotic offsets
- the showroom must remain easy to scan quickly

## Card Rhythm
- cards may vary in height based on preview length, summary presence, and tag density
- equal-width columns are acceptable, but equal-height rows are not the target
- occasional wider cards are acceptable only if they do not break readability or mobile collapse

## Content Priority
- title first
- short preview or summary second
- compact date/meta third
- tags last

## Anti-Goals
- no giant hero dominating above-the-fold space
- no rigid uniform archive wall
- no Pinterest-like randomness that hurts note scanning
